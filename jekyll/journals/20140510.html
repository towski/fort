Things can get pretty strange when you get to 30 nowadays. Lots of conflicting feelings. There is the desire to stay forever young. But there is also the feeling that you don't want to be doing all the dumb things young people are doing anymore.

There is the horrifying realization that people start noticing your age. Depending on your genes, you may continue to look appealing for a while. But the way you feel changes. It's not defeat to settle down. It's nature.

Like a fool, I personally felt like fighting nature. Like trying to continue to be young despite everything falling apart. Still wanting to found a crazy start-up. Get rich. Completely envious of my friends who were able to maintain that lifestyle.
