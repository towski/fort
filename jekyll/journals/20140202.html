I had a dream last night. A nerdy dream. A dream to stop splitting my identity into a million different pieces. To rebuild my own internet home. About keeping all my data on my own devices, and not letting the internet push and pull me around.

It feels like the internet has been going in the wrong direction. The excitement for the internet has died for a lot of people, because of the reality. The reality of centralized services, like honeypots, finding ways to get suckers to give them data. The more I think about it, the more I feel like sites like Facebook and Google are doing it all wrong. Taking and storing all the worlds information in one place is a waste of time and effort.

I think all users should be responsible for their own data. If someone wants to access their data, they should ask. It seems like centralized services are using the internet wrong. Publishing to the anonymous web is good for self promotion, but it should just be a replica of your own data. Stop giving away your data, and your time, for free. Centralized websites are wasting everyones time, to their own profit.
