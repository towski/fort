The Deepest Pain

Home Previous Next

This is the story of the deepest pain I have ever felt. A pain so deep, that it penetrated into the deepest core of my being, and shattered me. A pain that I couldn't brush off, or even explain. It's a pain that I still don't fully understand.

I always brushed pain off, avoided suffering. And felt reasonably secure in my world.

But then, I started to have my doubts. And I started having to question things. It made me question the trajectory I was taking in my life. It eventually it made me question everything.

See I never experienced a pain so deep that it breaks you. I had always been fairly fortunate. Things hadn't always gone my way. But I was fairly optimistic and good natured about it. I always felt it would be a waste to let the negativity swallow you. People that are negative are clearly damaged, right? I always recognized that and avoided them.

And I always felt like I was careful, even if I has made reckless decisions. But I started to realize that the dark side of the world was much bigger than I thought. All the things I thought would always be there, started to slip away. And I couldn't account for all the negativity I was surrounded with.

Sometimes a pain can get so deep that it shatters your very being. And overnight I went from being the optimistic good natured guy that I always enjoyed being, to a completely distraught, insecure, unhappy, confused person.

But tragedy happens.
