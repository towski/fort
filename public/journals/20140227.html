The necessity of personal adaptation

Previous Next Home

Human existence can be difficult to comprehend. We are born at a time. The world has changed drastically since then, and now we're here. The overall world population has increased a lot since you were born. Technology has changed, and so has industry. But more important than that, is your own personal adaptation.

You are either personally responding to the changes around you, or you are stuck in time. You either keep up with all the latest, or you fall behind.

It's scary and awesome to see these larger changes up close. I could practically watch buildings collapse in Chicago. Bask is the unchanging timelessness of Portland. Cruise through Los Angeles, which changes at a very human pace. And San Francisco, which is changing rapidly.

I personally had to adapt, or try to, in all of these places. And it terrified me that so many people are stuck in situations that can't control. And I got to a place where I couldn't adapt anymore. After 28 years I finally lost my cool. I couldn't pretend that everything was alright.
