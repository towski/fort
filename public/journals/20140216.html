Would it kill you to read some Stendhal?

It can be a little fatalistic and negative to read through the deepest depths of human experience, written down. Still, I can't help but feel a little disappointed by people. They don't seem to take any interest in the reality behind the fantasy.

Granted, it is a little much to ask other people to learn french. Or to read realistic books. But still, I can't help but feel isolated by my own interests.

Really no one I know has read the Red and the Black? There is something about identifying deeply with something that really plumbs the depths.
